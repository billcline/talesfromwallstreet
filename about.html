About the Author

Bill Cline

Former professional musician, trader, trading system entrepreneur, and global consulting managing partner Bill Cline was a pioneer in the digital transformation of Wall Street and capital markets. He is widely recognized for product and service innovations in trading, real-time data, new electronic exchanges, global outsourcing and managed services, and AI-enabled process automation. Bill has a 40-year history of transforming capital markets, survived 9/11 against the odds, and had an inside view of the strategies and political intrigue leading to the separation of Andersen Consulting from Arthur Andersen, rebranding, and the blockbuster Accenture IPO.
